Bio

I am a Research Assistant in Mathematics at Imperial College London, working in the group of Dr Michele Coti Zelati.
I did my PhD studies at Gran Sasso Science Institute, L’Aquila, under the supervision of Dr Michele Coti Zelati.

Research

My main research interests lie on the analysis of partial differential equation, particularly focused on problems related to the fluid dynamics.
